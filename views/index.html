Course Watch |

  • You must verify your CRN before making a request, the red button will turn green when you're good.
  • After you make a request, system will check on your seat every two minutes.
  • You will be e-mailed and/or texted as soon as the system realizes a seat is open.
  • Everything will automatically update and be ready when a new term comes around.
  • Unfortunately, the sms feature has been spotty with some carriers.

Note

  • You do not have to be logged in to use this feature, but you should be if you want the ability to cancel your request later.
  • When multiple people request course watches on the same CRN, notifications are sent out to everyone at the same time to be fair.

Automated Registration |

  • Verify your CRN & BuzzPort on this page before making a request; the red button will turn green when you're good.
  • When the system realizes a seat is free, it will log in to BuzzPort and register your class for you; additionally, you'll receive a registration-success e-mail notification.
  • If multiple people request the same CRN, they are registered on a first come first served basis as seats open up.
  • Your information is stored on a secure server, and is deleted once either your request expires or is fulfilled.

Requirements

  • You must be signed in to this website to use the auto-registration feature.
  • You can cancel your request anytime under Account -> My Requests in the top bar.
